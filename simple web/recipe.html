dear prudence

ingredients

martini glass rimmed with cinnimon sugar

  • 1.5oz spiced rum
  • 1oz creme de cacao
  • 1oz chai syrup
  • .25oz orange juice
  • .25oz vanilla
  • drop of vegan egg whites
  • cinnamon sugar

    • step by step

    1

    begin by chilling a martini glass with ice to chill.

    2

    combine ingredients: pour the rum, chocolate liqueur, chai, orange juice, and vanilla into a cocktail shaker.

    3

    before shaking the ingredients, rim the side of the chilled martini glass with cinnimon sugar, inside and out.

    4

    add the egg whites and shake vigorously: fill the shaker with ice, seal, and shake for at least 15–30 seconds until the outside of the shaker is frosty. This is the crucial step to create the foam.

    5

    strain: double strain the mixture (using a shaker strainer and a fine-mesh sieve) into your chilled glass.

    6

    garnish: the foam should have cinnamon sugar floating, use a tooth pick to swirl the sugar around the glass.

    7

    enjoy.

    drink responsibly.
